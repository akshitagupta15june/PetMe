HOW A SIMPLE THING CAN BRING BIG CHANGE TO OUR LIFE

Shruti Sen

October 16, 2023

This story unfolds in the tumultuous year of 2020, marked by the infamous COVID-19 pandemic. At that time, I was a student in Class 11, attending a CBSE board school. One fine day, my school assigned a project with a social awareness theme. We had a variety of topics to choose from, and I decided to focus on "Helping Street Dogs." Given the abundance of stray dogs in my neighborhood, I initially thought it would be a straightforward project.

To kickstart my project, I purchased 15 packets of biscuits and set out to feed the street dogs. While I was feeding them, I couldn't help but gaze into their eyes, and it was as though they were expressing gratitude for the food. The connection I felt with these dogs left a lasting impression on me. Upon returning home and while I was writing about my project, I had a profound realization. In the midst of the pandemic, it became evident that humans weren't the only ones suffering. While we were confined to our homes, there was a noticeable absence of care and attention given to these poor, helpless dogs. This realization deeply saddened me.

From that moment forward, whenever I encountered a dog in need, I felt compelled to come to their aid. The joy radiating from their smiles and the enthusiasm of their wagging tails was a heartwarming reward. These experiences have since inspired me to be a staunch advocate for the welfare of street dogs. This story serves as a poignant reminder that even in the midst of challenging times, small acts of kindness can make a significant difference in the lives of those who cannot speak for themselves.
