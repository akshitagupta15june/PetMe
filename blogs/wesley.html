Kafka's blog:

Wesley Vinson

October 8, 2022

Today, I'm writing down this blog to introduce you to Kafka, my cat. Though, we rarely use this name. It's either Bibou or Bibouch.

Kafka is a peculiar cat, which is a very common attribute within cats. I have to mention, that he is both aggressive and annoying. As a cat owner, I spend some days feeling sleep deprived. Still, I can't express how much joy he showers me with every day.

I remember when we found him in the street, me and my sister. He was too small and meowing, and as soon as we lie our eyes upon him, we agreed to take him home with us. I took him in my arms. He instantly stopped meowing, and he was so quiet all the way home. Except I think that was some acting. He's definitely one of the loudest cats I ever had. Though, I'm sure my life would have been too boring without him.

We had passed the pandemic together and the different quarantine. I graduated and went to a new university. We did spend many days together, and Bibou did change (still violent, though) but we also did grow up together. Thank you for the last 2 years and a half, we love you.

PS: My cat is very energised and crazy today, he woke me up at 7 am, I think he knows I'm writing some things about him.
